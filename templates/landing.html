Transforming Indian Poultry Sector

Under the visionary leadership of our government, India's poultry sector has emerged as a vital component of agricultural economy. With over 851 million birds and production of 138.38 billion eggs annually, we rank 3rd globally in egg production. Our comprehensive schemes like National Livestock Mission provide 50% subsidies up to Rs. 25 lakh, supporting farmers with modern infrastructure. The Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund ensures 90% loan coverage with 3% interest subvention, empowering over 1 million broiler farmers and creating employment for 5 million people across the value chain.
